Subpopulations in ASD & ADHD

In collaboration with Washington University in St. Louis, we are studying children ages 9-10 to better understand the differences in the brains of those with and without ADHD and with and without Autism. Data will be collected throughout 6 study visits involving the following:

  • Questionnaires
  • Cognitive Tasks
  • fMRIs 
  • Optional DNA biospecimen (saliva)

Participation will last several months and families will be compensated for their time. To learn more about this study, please contact Nora Byington (bying015@umn.edu).
